How to Play

Objective

Score points by sliding discs into the scoring zones at the opposite end of the board.

Controls

  • WASD/Arrow Keys: Move camera
  • Mouse: Look around
  • Left Click & Hold: Charge shot
  • Release Left Click: Shoot disc
  • ESC: Toggle menu

Scoring

  • Yellow Zone: 10 points
  • Blue Zone: 8 points
  • Green Zone: 7 points
  • Red Zone: -10 points

First player to reach 75 points wins!
